History of the World

History is the study of past events, particularly in human affairs. From ancient civilizations like Mesopotamia and Egypt to the modern digital age, history helps us understand how society has evolved over time.

Key Periods in History

  • Ancient History – 3000 BCE to 500 CE
  • Middle Ages – 500 to 1500 CE
  • Modern History – 1500 to present
